The Julius K9 IDC® (Innovative Dog Control) Powerharness is the perfect choice for everyday use. It’s popular thanks to its heavy-duty, secure, easy-to-use, clear and clean design.
The Powerharness is designed for every dog - including yours. For optimum load distribution, the size and position of the harness' chest strap is based on mathematical calculations.

  • Front chest strap safely distributes tension on the breast muscle. This is the strongest part of your dog's body.
  • All materials are certified by OEKO-TEX® for the safety and comfort of your dog
  • Tensile-tested German strap
  • Fastening elements manufactured from Swiss materials
  • Its unique harness design offers maximum freedom of movement
  • Every harness is handmade in the EU
