Petsafe Little Dog Containment System  PIG22-11041

Maintenance free design and easy to install. Designed for small dogs and comes with the smallest QuickFit receiver collar that provides a quick release and perfect fit every time.  Collar has 4 correction levels and is totally waterproof. This dog fence system can cover properties up to 25 acres with additional PetSafe Boundary Kits. Any number of Little Dog Extra Collars can be added at any time.

Note: Any other PetSafe Wired Containment Fence Collar can be added to this dog fence system if you have dogs that are better suited to different collars because of their size or temperament.
